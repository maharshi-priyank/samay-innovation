# Award Images

## How to Add Award Images

Place your award images in this folder with the following names:

1. **UK Award 2022**: `uk-award-2022.jpg`
   - Award: Most Promising Interior Designer In Asia and UK
   - Location: House Of Commons, London UK
   - Year: May 2022
   - Recommended size: 800x600px or 4:3 aspect ratio

2. **India Award 2019**: `india-award-2019.jpg`
   - Award: Best Green Compliant Interior Designer In India
   - Location: Taj Bangalore
   - Year: December 2019
   - Recommended size: 800x600px or 4:3 aspect ratio

## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:3 (landscape)
- **Recommended Size**: 800x600px minimum
- **Max File Size**: 500KB for optimal loading
- **Quality**: High resolution, well-lit, professional photos
